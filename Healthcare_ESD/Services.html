Maternal Health Services

Comprehensive Care for Mothers

Our maternal health program provides essential care for women before, during, and after pregnancy. We focus on making motherhood safer in rural areas where access to obstetric care is limited.

  • Prenatal checkups and education
  • Safe delivery support
  • Postnatal care for mothers and newborns
  • Family planning counseling
  • High-risk pregnancy management

Preventive Care Services

Staying Healthy Through Prevention

We believe prevention is better than cure. Our preventive services help rural women avoid common health issues through screenings, vaccinations, and education.

  • Routine health screenings
  • Vaccination programs
  • Cancer screenings (breast, cervical)
  • Health education workshops
  • Hygiene and sanitation education

Chronic Condition Management

Managing Long-Term Health Issues

We provide ongoing support for women dealing with chronic health conditions, helping them manage their symptoms and improve quality of life.

  • Diabetes management programs
  • Hypertension monitoring and care
  • Arthritis and joint pain management
  • Respiratory disease care
  • Medication management support

Mental Wellness Services

Caring for Mental Health

Mental health is as important as physical health. Our program addresses the unique psychological challenges faced by rural women.

  • Depression and anxiety screening
  • Counseling services
  • Stress management workshops
  • Domestic violence support
  • Community support groups
Find Mental Health Support

Nutrition Guidance

Healthy Eating for Better Living

We provide practical nutrition education using locally available foods to improve health outcomes in rural communities.

  • Nutritional assessments
  • Pregnancy and lactation nutrition
  • Managing diet for chronic conditions
  • Cooking demonstrations
  • Home gardening guidance
Find Nutrition Resources

Telemedicine Services

Healthcare at Your Fingertips

When distance is a barrier, our telemedicine program connects rural women with doctors through simple technology.

  • Remote consultations with specialists
  • Medication prescriptions
  • Follow-up care without travel
  • Health monitoring support
  • Available via smartphone or computer
